The Problem We See

Students—especially in final year—often hit a wall: they need real-world experience to get a job, but many internships are paid, fake, or offer no real work. Part-time opportunities that fit around college are rare. Theory-heavy education leaves a gap between what you learn and what the industry needs.

We started DeepTrics because we believe you shouldn’t have to pay for experience, and you shouldn’t have to settle for work that doesn’t count.

Why We Exist

DeepTrics was not started for money. It was started to fix a real problem: giving students real opportunities instead of empty certificates.

We focus on real projects, real teams, and real learning. Our goal is to help you become someone who can walk into an interview with confidence—because you’ve already built things that matter.

How It Works

Three simple steps from joining to becoming interview-ready.

1

Join for free

Apply through our website. No fees, no hidden costs. We review your application and onboard you into a team.

2

Work on real projects with teams

You contribute to real software—our own products or client work. You use industry tools and follow real workflows. You collaborate like in a real company.

3

Become interview-ready

You leave with real experience, a portfolio, and the confidence to talk about what you’ve built. Final-year students can also earn while working on real products.

What You Gain

Not just a certificate—things that actually help you in interviews and on the job.

  • Real project experience — Shipped code, not dummy tasks.
  • Portfolio — Work you can show to recruiters and hiring managers.
  • Confidence — Knowing you’ve done real work in a team.
  • Industry tools & workflows — Git, agile practices, and how products are built.
  • Interview readiness — Stories and projects to talk about.

This is the kind of experience recruiters actually ask about.

How We Keep It Free

You might wonder how internships can be free. Here’s how it works.

DeepTrics builds its own software products and also works with real clients. Revenue from product development and client work funds student internships and learning. That’s how the program stays sustainable—and stays free for students. No fees from you, no compromise on real work.

This model allows us to stay independent, ethical, and student-first.
