About DeepTrics

At DeepTrics, we're more than just a team—we're a movement of passionate developers, educators, and innovators on a mission to transform student learning! Our student-first platform is designed to ignite real-world skills through 100% free, hands-on internships that go beyond the classroom. By bridging the gap between theory and industry, we empower students to build confidence, sharpen their talents, and step boldly into the future.

Our Motto

Empowering Students, One Real-World Project at a Time.

We believe learning is most impactful when paired with hands-on application. That’s why our programs focus on meaningful experiences that prepare students for the real world.

What We Offer

  • Free Internships Opportunities in Web Development, App Development, and UI/UX Design that mirror real industry workflows—complete with teamwork, deadlines, and version control.
  • Real-World Projects Interns work on live projects for startups, NGOs, or internal tools—gaining tangible outcomes for their portfolios and understanding real business environments.
  • Mentorship & Collaboration Guidance from experienced mentors, peer collaboration, and exposure to Agile practices and Git version control.
  • Certificates & Recognition Verified internship certificates, personal feedback, and a platform to showcase projects to potential employers.
